About Me

As a web developer, I enjoy solving problems, working with creative, passionate people, and constantly learning new things. My favorite projects incorporate data, clear communication, and easy access to information.

I am looking for a web development position where I can combine my previous project management experiences with my technical skills and enthusiasm for life-long learning.

In my previous administrative and events management roles, I created systems that fostered efficient, clear communication; I looked at every detail of an event so the team was set up for success. I chose to pursue web development because I want a more challenging and fulfilling career where I can make a difference in the field of education.

I studied web development at General Assembly, where I built several full-stack applications in Ruby on Rails and postgreSQL, Javascript with Node, Express and MongoDB, and AngularJS. I have experience working independently and in teams, using the agile development process to develop each feature or design layer efficiently and incorporating feedback about user experience.

When I'm not programming, I can be found volunteering with the Boys and Girls Clubs of San Francisco, cooking, hiking, traveling, doing yoga, and visiting art museums.
